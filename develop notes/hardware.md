This kind of board is very suitable

- [LoRa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LoRa)
- [FSK](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frequency-shift_keying)

- [BLE 5.0](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bluetooth_Low_Energy)
